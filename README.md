E-Commerce Sales & Revenue Analytics System
📌 Overview

The E-Commerce Sales & Revenue Analytics System is a data analytics project developed to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, product trends, and business profitability using Python, SQL, and visualization tools. The project transforms raw transactional data into meaningful business insights through data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), SQL analytics, and dashboard development.

This project demonstrates practical skills in data analytics, business intelligence, and reporting suitable for Data Analyst and Business Analyst roles.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze sales and revenue trends
Identify profitable products and categories
Evaluate customer purchasing behavior
Monitor regional sales performance
Perform business KPI analysis
Generate actionable business insights
Build interactive dashboards for reporting
